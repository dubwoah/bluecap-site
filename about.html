WHAT WE DO

For Startups

We partner with technical and product-focused startup teams to help them land and scale inside the enterprise. We offer hands-on guidance around positioning, go-to-market strategy, and enterprise readiness — and we help shape the product to match real buyer needs.

  • Go-to-market positioning for AI and deep-tech startups
  • Sales and growth strategy for long-cycle, multi-stakeholder deals
  • Enterprise integration guidance: security, compliance, deployment
  • Founder coaching: customer calls, sales support, investor alignment

For Enterprise

We help enterprise teams adopt and scale AI initiatives with clarity and impact. From discovery to deployment, we work cross-functionally to ensure alignment, implementation, and measurable outcomes — not just slideware.

  • AI strategy design: opportunity mapping, use case evaluation
  • Pilot programs: vendor selection, internal onboarding, KPIs & metrics
  • Org-wide alignment: workshops, cross-functional collaboration
  • Execution support: embedded project leadership, roll-out strategy, tooling alignment

PHILOSOPHY

We believe AI should amplify human ability — not replace it. The best systems make people faster, smarter, and more effective — whether you're a founder building from scratch or a leader driving transformation at scale.

We don’t get caught up in hype. We focus on what works: strategy grounded in business outcomes, execution aligned with real constraints, and tools that actually get adopted.

For startups, that means building with enterprise reality in mind from day one. For enterprises, it means deploying AI where it creates durable value — not dashboards that gather dust. In both cases, our work is human-centered, outcome-driven, and built to last.
